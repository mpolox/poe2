## Crafting Cost Breakdown

### Cost per Craft Type

#### Omen Crafting
- Single Omen Cost: 1-5c (average 3c per omen)
- Multi-Omen Sequence (2-3 omens): 5-20c
- Estimated Attempts for Success: 1.5-2.5 attempts
- **Total Cost per Item: 10-30c for quality crafts**

#### Currency Crafting
- Alteration Cost: 0.01c (1 per 100c)
- Annulment Cost: 80-100c each
- Exalt Cost: 180-200c each
- Divine Cost: 250-300c each
