## Omen Categories

### Tier 1: Unique/Powerful Omens

#### Omen of Prosperity
- **Effect**: Adds a powerful prefix to an item
- **Rarity**: Unique
- **Acquisition Cost**: 15-20c
- **Best Used On**: Mid-tier bases, chests, weapons
- **Priority**: Critical

#### Omen of Swiftness
- **Effect**: Adds high Movement Speed suffix
- **Rarity**: Unique
- **Acquisition Cost**: 12-15c

#### Omen of Determination
- **Effect**: Adds Armour/Evasion modifiers

## Omen Synergies

**Best Combinations (Synergy):**
- Omen of Swiftness + Omen of Resistance = Boots powerhouse
- Omen of Prosperity + Omen of Precision = Weapon damage dealer
- Omen of Defiance + Omen of Vitality = Defensive tank items
- Omen of Velocity + Omen of Firepower = Attack-based weapons

**DON'T Mix (Conflicting):**
- Omen of Firepower + Omen of Frostbite (conflicting damage types)
- Multiple Movement Speed omens (won't stack effectively)
- Too many defensive omens (wasted value)

**Budget Friendly:**
- Omen of Vitality + Omen of Fortitude (cheapest effective combo)
- Omen of Resistance + any damage omen (good value)

**High Value:**
- Omen of Prosperity + specialized damage omen (80+ chaos items)
- Triple omen combos (rare but powerful results)
