## Recommended Omen Combinations

### Combo 1: Resistances + Stats (Universal)
**Omen Sequence:** Omen of Resistance → Omen of Prosperity
**Cost:** 25-30c per craft
**Success Rate:** 58%
**Expected Sell:** 140-165c
**Profit:** 99-120c ✅ BEST OPTION (Rings)

Best on: Iron Ring (16c base), Sapphire Ring (20c base)
Why: Everyone needs resistances + stats; consistent demand

**Expected Sell:** 160-180c
