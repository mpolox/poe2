## Recommended Omen Combinations

### Combo 1: Max Life + Resistances (Tank Build)
**Omen Sequence:** Omen of Defiance → Omen of Resistance
**Cost:** 28-35c per craft
**Success Rate:** 58%
**Expected Sell:** 120-130c
**Profit:** 75-95c ✅ BEST OPTION

Best on: Armor Plate (12c base), Siege Armor (14c base)
Why: Tank builds need survivability; highest absolute profit

---

### Combo 2: Spell Damage + Intelligence (Caster)
**Omen Sequence:** Omen of Burning → Omen of Intellect
**Cost:** 20-24c per craft
**Success Rate:** 65%
**Expected Sell:** 85-95c
**Profit:** 58-70c ✅ SOLID OPTION

Best on: Destroyer Chest (6c base), War Plate (5c base)
Why: Spell meta strong; solid profit on mid-tier bases

### Combo 3: Physical Damage + Health (Budget Warrior)

**Expected Sell:** 50-60c

Best on: Simple Robe (2c base)
Why: Very low barrier, warrior builds active, good volume
