## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Too Many Failed Attempts
- **Solution**: Switch to lower-tier bases with simpler combos
- Reduce omen count to 1-2
- Use budget strategy

### Issue: Can't Sell Crafted Boots
- **Solution**: Price too high - reduce target price by 10-20%
- Check if base implicit matches current meta
- Try different omen combo

### Issue: Profit Margin Too Low
- **Solution**: Buy bases cheaper (wait for price drop)
- Use cheaper omen combinations
- Focus on higher-tier bases instead

### Issue: Out of Currency
- **Solution**: Sell failed/partial crafts to NPCs for refund
- Do lower-budget runs on Tier 3 bases
- Save up 20c before next crafting session

## Tips for Maximum Profit

1. **Monitor price trends** - Buy bases when cheap, craft when demand peaks
2. **Batch craft** - Buying 5 bases and crafting together is faster
3. **Omen combos** - Stick to proven combos (find success rate > 50%)
4. **Implicit matters** - Check base implicit - some have built-in value
5. **Market timing** - Weekend = higher demand, better prices
6. **Keep records** - Track what worked to improve future crafts
