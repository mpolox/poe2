## Recommended Omen Combinations

### Combo 1: Physical Damage + Critical (Attack Builds)
**Omen Sequence:** Omen of Slaughter → Omen of Fortune

**Success Rate:** 60%
**Expected Sell:** 85-95c
**Profit:** 55-70c ✅ BEST OPTION (One-Handed)
